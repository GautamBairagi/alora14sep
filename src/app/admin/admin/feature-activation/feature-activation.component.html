CareConnect/EVV Activated

CareConnect brings software solutions to all your caregivers, including aides and PCWs, through their smartphones, tablets, and laptops.

For Caregivers: Your caregivers can access their schedules, capture the start and end times of visits, complete patient-specific POC-based visit notes, and capture patient and caregiver signatures.

For the Agency: Your office staff can monitor live visits as they occur and see delays and no-shows with up-to-the-minute updates. Visits completed as planned are automatically updated on the scheduler and become instantly ready for billing and payroll.

EVV: Home Care is designated as an alternate EVV vendor in many states. And in many others, we are waiting for the states to announce their EVV requirements so that we can tailor our EVV solution to meet state regulations. When EVV is enabled, CareConnect serves as your EVV solution. Data captured via CareConnect is transmitted to the state EVV aggregator.
